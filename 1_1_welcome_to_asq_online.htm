1.1 Welcome to ASQ Online

ASQ Online is a web-based management system for the Ages & Stages Questionnaires® that makes it easier for ASQ users to manage and track child data. ASQ Online has all the integrity of ASQ-3, ASQ:SE and ASQ:SE-2 plus the convenience of automated scoring, and a powerful functionality that transforms ASQ findings into child screening reports. 

We are excited for you to see all the ways ASQ Online can make it easier for you to help your children make real progress.
