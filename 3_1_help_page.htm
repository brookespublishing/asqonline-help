3.1 Home Page

Immediately after you log in to your ASQ Online account, you will be directed to your Home page, which is personalized according to your role within the system. It is from this page that you will have quick access to the important information you need to manage your ASQ Online account, including Account Snapshot, Announcements, Resource Corner, and Quick Links.

Account Snapshot

On the bottom left corner of your Home page is the Account Snapshot section. Within this section you can see up-to-date stats and a snapshot of your account, including the total number of programs in your account, the number of users, the number of Child Profiles, and the number of finalized screenings.

Announcements

At the top of your Home page, you will see the Announcements section. Here, the ASQ Online Implementation team will provide news and updates on new ASQ Online features, information on professional development as well as tips and tricks for using the online system.
