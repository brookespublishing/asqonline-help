3.4 Your Role: Provider

As a Provider, you are responsible for managing the screening of children to whom you are assigned. Your main responsibilities will include entering in child and caregiver information, entering screening results for your children, and making decisions regarding future screenings, follow-up, and referrals.

You also have the ability to create tasks for your children as well as the ability to create and organize documents and other materials that you will use for screenings, follow-ups, and referrals. You have the ability to generate reports for children to whom you are assigned.

If you are a Family Access subscriber, as a Provider, you will be also be able to design Family Access pages that you will send to the caregivers in your program so that they can fill out ASQ questionnaires online.
