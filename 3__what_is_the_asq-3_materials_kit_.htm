3. What is the ASQ-3 Materials Kit?

The ASQ-3 Materials Kit contains the items needed to do ASQ screenings at every age interval. The ASQ-3 Materials Kit consists of toys, books, and other items designed to encourage a child’s participation and support effective, accurate administration of questionnaires.
