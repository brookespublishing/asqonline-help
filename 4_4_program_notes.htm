4.4 Program Notes

The Program Notes section allows you to enter and store notes pertaining to your program.

To add a note:

1.      Select Program from the top navigation menu.

2.      Click on the Add tab next to Program Notes.

3.      Complete the following:

a.      Enter in the subject of the note.

b.      Select the note type from the drop-down: Reminder, Contact Information, Goal, Procedures, or Other.

c.       Enter the text of the note in the “Description” text box.

4.      Click Save.
