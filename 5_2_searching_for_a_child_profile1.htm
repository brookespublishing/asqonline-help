5.2 Searching for a Child Profile

To search for a specific child or children to whom you are assigned:
  1. Select Child Profiles from the top navigation menu.
  2. Search for a child or children by entering information in any of the following search fields: Child DOB, Last Updated Date, Date Created, Child last name, Child first name, Child ID, Previous screening (select the screening result from the drop-down menu), Caregiver last name, Caregiver first name, and/or Alt. ID.
  3. Click Search. 
  4. Once you have located the child you are looking for, click on the child’s name to view his or her profile.
