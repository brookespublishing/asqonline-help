6.13 Requesting Child Transfers (Pro account only)

If you are the Program Administrator of a Pro account, you have the ability to request a child transfer to another account in ASQ Online. When a child is transferred, that child is deleted from the transferring program or account and added to the receiving program or account. This transfer will be done at the System Administrator level following a request.

The following data will transfer with a child:

 Child Profile

  • Caregiver Profile(s)
  • Child’s screenings
  • Child notes
  • Completed tasks
  • List of child’s Providers & Reviewers
  • Transfer history

 To request a child transfer to another ASQ Online account:

 

  1. Select Child Profiles from the top navigation menu.
  2. You can search for the child by using the Child Profile Search section at the top of the page. You can search by Child last name, Child first name, Child ID, and/or Alt. ID. Or, you can simply locate the child from the Child Profiles list.
  3. Click the Add to Transfer link to the right of the child profiles you want to transfer.  You can transfer multiple children at one time. If you are transferring more than 1 child to the same account, click the Add to Transfer link next to each child you wish to transfer to that account.  
  4. Click Start Transfer at the bottom of the page when done selecting child profiles to transfer.
  5. Click the "Click here to request a transfer from your account to another ASQ Online account."
  6. Enter the Account Name and Program Name of the Destination in the corresponding fields.
  7. If you don’t wish to proceed with this transfer request, click the Cancel button. You will be taken back to the Child Profiles page.
  8. If you wish to proceed with this transfer, click the Request Transfer button.
  9. You will be taken back the Child Profiles page. You will receive a message that the transfer has been requested.

 

You can view any pending transfers by clicking the Pending Transfers tab on the Transfer Child Profiles page.
