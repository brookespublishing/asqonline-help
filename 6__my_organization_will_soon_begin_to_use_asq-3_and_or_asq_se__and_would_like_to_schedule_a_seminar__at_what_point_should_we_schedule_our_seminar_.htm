6. My organization will soon begin to use ASQ-3 and/or ASQ:SE-2, and would like to schedule a seminar. At what point should we schedule our seminar?

We recommend trying to schedule two months or more in advance. For best results, we recommend that all of your participants read the User’s Guides and practice using the questionnaires for 3-6 months, before scheduling a seminar.

If you would like to schedule a seminar with Brookes On Location, please contact us via www.brookesonlocation.com or seminars@brookespublishing.com.
