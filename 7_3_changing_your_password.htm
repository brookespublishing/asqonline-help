7.3 Changing Your Password

To change your password:

 

1.      Select My Profile from the top navigation menu.

2.      Click on the Edit tab to the right of the My Profile tab.

3.      Type in a new password.

4.      In the “Password confirmation” field, retype the new password.

5.      Click the Save button.
