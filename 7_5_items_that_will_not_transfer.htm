7.5 Items That Will Not Transfer

Not all items from a Child Profile will transfer with that child. If an item will not transfer, you will receive a message detailing those items when you preview a transfer. You can still transfer the child, with the understanding that the items outlined will not transfer.

The following items will not transfer with a child:


  • Incomplete tasks
  • Screenings where the receiving program does not have the corresponding keycode
  • Completed screening tasks that haven’t been returned
  • Custom fields (when transferring from account-to-account only)
