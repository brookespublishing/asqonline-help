7. How do I change my password in ASQ Online?

To change your password in ASQ Online:

1.      Select My Profile from the top navigation menu.

2.      Click the Edit tab, to the right of the My Profile tab.

3.      Scroll down to the bottom of the page where you will see the fields “Password” and “Password confirmation.”

4.      Type your new password in the “Password” field. (Note: The password must be at least 6 characters long.)

5.      Retype the password in the “Password confirmation” field.

6.      Click the Save button.

7.      Be sure to write down your username and password so that you can keep track of it.

Note: Passwords are case sensitive.
