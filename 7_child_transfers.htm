7 Child Transfers

As Account Administrator, you have the ability to transfer child data between programs in your account, as well as the ability to request child transfers between accounts. When transferring a child, you are deleting that child from the transferring program or account and adding the child to the receiving program or account.  

The following data will transfer with a child:
  • Child Profile 
  • Caregiver Profile(s) 
  • Child's screenings
  • Child notes 
  • Completed tasks 
  • List of child’s Providers & Reviewers 
  • Transfer history
