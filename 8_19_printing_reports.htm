9.19 Printing Reports

When you generate a report as a PDF file, all reports will be 8.5 x 14.

In order to print the report on 8.5 x 11 paper:

1.      After generating the report and opening as a PDF (in Adobe Reader), select “File” -> “Print.”

2.      In the Page Handling section, select the “Fit to Printable Area” option from the Page Scaling drop-down menu.

3.      Click the OK button.
