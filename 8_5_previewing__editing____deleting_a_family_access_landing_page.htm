8.5 Previewing, Editing, & Deleting a Family Access Landing Page

Once you’ve created your Family Access landing page, you can preview what it looks like and make any desired changes. You can also delete the page entirely.

To preview what the landing page will look like to your caregivers:

 

  1. Select Family Access from the top navigation menu.
  2. Click the Show link to the right of the Family Access URL to preview the page.

To edit your Family Access landing page:

  1. Select Family Access from the top navigation menu.
  2. Click the Edit link to the right of the Family Access URL.
  3. Make any desired changes.
  4. Click the Update button at the bottom of the page.

 

To delete your Family Access landing page:

  1. Select Family Access from the top navigation menu.
  2. Click the Delete link to the right of the Family Access URL.
