8.8 Family Access Child List

If you wish to see a list of all children for whom you have printed or emailed the invitation letter, you can click the Child List link to the right of the Family Access URL. The Child List page will show you each child for whom that letter has been sent, the child’s date of birth, and the date the letter was generated for that child.

You can also search for specific children by date generated, child DOB, child name, child ID, and child alt. ID.

To view a packet’s Child List:

  1. Select Family Access from the top navigation menu.
  2. Click the Child List link to the right of the Family Access landing page URL.
