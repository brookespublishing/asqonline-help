9.2 Recent Reports

On the Reports page, you will see two tabs: Available Reports and Recent Reports. The Recent Reports section will list all reports that you have generated within the last week, with the option to Preview, Download PDF, or Download CSV. This allows you to see your most recently generated reports without having to select the search criteria and generate the report again.
