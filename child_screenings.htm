7 Child Screenings

As a Program Administrator, you have the ability to enter screening results for the children in your program,  The child must have an assigned Provider.  You may also view, print, and delete screenings. You can add screenings from a child’s profile page, and you can manage screenings from the child’s profile page or the Screenings page.
